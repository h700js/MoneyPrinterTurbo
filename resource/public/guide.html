1. 🤖 What is Kreator AI?

Kreator AI is an AI-powered video generator that creates short videos automatically. You type a topic — like "Top 10 AI Tools 2026" — and it handles everything:

  • 🧠 Writes the script using AI (DeepSeek, OpenAI, Gemini, etc.)
  • 🎥 Finds video clips from Pexels matching your topic
  • 🗣️ Creates voiceover using text-to-speech technology
  • 📝 Adds subtitles with customizable fonts and colors
  • 🎵 Adds background music automatically
  • ⚡ Renders a complete HD video ready to upload
💡 Who is this for? TikTok & Reels creators, marketers, educators, news channels — anyone who needs quality short videos fast.

2. ⚡ Quick Start — First Video in 3 Minutes

Step 1: Type a topic in the main input field
Step 2: Click "Generate Video Script and Keywords"
Step 3: Review the AI-generated script (edit if needed)
Step 4: Click "Generate Video" — done!

The app starts in Simple Mode by default — you only see the essential fields. If you want more control over video/audio/subtitle settings, switch to Advanced Mode in the sidebar.

3. 🎛️ Simple vs Advanced Mode

Open the sidebar (click the arrow top-left). You'll see a mode toggle:

All settings have smart defaults — even in Simple Mode you'll get a polished video with subtitles, voice, and music automatically.

5. ✍️ Writing Scripts with AI

Good Topics = Good Results

  • ❌ Bad: "AI"
  • ✅ Good: "Top 10 AI Tools That Will Change Your Life in 2026"
  • ✅ Good: "How to Make a Perfect Chocolate Cake in 5 Minutes"

Language

Advanced Script Settings

6. 🎬 Video, Audio & Subtitle Settings

Available in Advanced Mode only.

Video Settings

  • Video Source: Pexels (recommended), Pixabay, or Local files
  • Video Ratio: Portrait 9:16 (TikTok/Reels) or Landscape 16:9 (YouTube)
  • Clip Duration: 3-5 seconds per clip recommended
  • Transitions: None, Fade, Slide — pick your style

Audio Settings

  • TTS Server: Azure TTS V1 (free, default)
  • Voice: Choose from dozens of natural voices
  • Background Music: Random or custom

Subtitle Settings

  • Enable/Disable: Toggle on/off
  • Font, Size, Color: Fully customizable
  • Position: Top, Center, Bottom, or Custom %

7. 🚀 Generating & Downloading

Click the big "Generate Video" button. The process takes 1-3 minutes. After generation, a video player appears — watch in browser or download the MP4 file. Videos are saved in the storage/tasks/ folder.

8. 💡 Tips & Best Practices

  • Be specific with your topic — more details = better script
  • Use Simple Mode if you're just starting
  • Test different voices — some sound more natural than others
  • Keep music at 20-30% volume so voice stays clear
  • Enable subtitles — many viewers watch without sound
  • Generate 3-5 videos at once for content variety

9. 🔧 Troubleshooting

"Please Enter the LLM API Key"

Go to Basic Settings, select an LLM Provider, and paste your API key.

"Please Enter the Pexels API Key"

In Basic Settings → Video Source, paste your Pexels API key.

Video generation fails / stuck

  • Check internet connection
  • Verify API keys are correct
  • Try a shorter topic with fewer keywords
  • Check the log panel for details

No voice / audio

  • Make sure TTS is not set to "No Voice" (Advanced Mode)
  • Check speech volume is not zero

10. ❓ FAQ

The software is free. You only need free API keys (DeepSeek + Pexels) which give free credits. Zero cost to start.

Short videos (30-90 seconds) for TikTok, Reels, YouTube Shorts, educational content, news, product reviews, and more.

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything runs on your own server. API keys stay on your machine.

Pengaturan Video

Pengaturan Subtitle
