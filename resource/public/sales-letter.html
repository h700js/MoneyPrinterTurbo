🤖 What is MoneyPrinterTurbo?

MoneyPrinterTurbo is an open-source AI-powered short video generator. Simply provide a topic or keyword, and it will automatically:

  • 🧠 Generate video scripts using LLM (GPT, Claude, DeepSeek, etc.)
  • 🎥 Fetch high-quality video materials from Pexels / Pixabay
  • 📝 Add beautiful subtitles with customizable fonts & colors
  • 🎵 Synthesize natural voiceover with Azure TTS / SiliconFlow / Gemini TTS
  • 🎶 Add background music automatically
  • ⚡ Render a complete HD short video — ready to post!
